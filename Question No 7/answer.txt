Question :: 
        Explain the 3-schema architecture along with its advantages.
Answer :: 
        The 3-schema architecture consists of three levels:
            1. External Schema (User View): Describes how users perceive the data.
            2. Conceptual Schema (Community View): Represents the logical structure of the entire database.
            3. Internal Schema (Physical View): Describes how data is stored and accessed at the physical level.
