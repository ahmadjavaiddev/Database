Question :: 
        What is the significance of Data Modeling, and explain the types of Data Modeling.
Answer :: 
        Data Modeling is crucial in database design, representing the structure of the data and the relationships between different elements. The two main types of Data Modeling are:
            1. Conceptual Data Modeling: Focuses on high-level abstraction and relationships between entities.
            2. Logical Data Modeling: Defines the specific data elements, their relationships, and attributes, translating the conceptual model into a detailed representation.
